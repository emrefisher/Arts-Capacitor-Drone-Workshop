Flightpath Creator 2.0

  • The three grids below represent different views of your flight space.
  • To start a flightpath, add a point to any of the three grids below by double-clicking on the desired location in one of the grids.
  • When you want to move a previously added point, click and drag the point to its new location and release the mouse button.
  • If you want to clear your path, press the Clear Path button at the top of the screen.
  • When you are ready to export your path, press the Create CSV button at the top of the screen to download a CSV file of your flightpath.
